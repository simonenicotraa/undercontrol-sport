With UnderControl you manage your Society with a few clicks

What is UnderControl..?

This web-app was born from the need to put order in the desks of the offices of sports clubs. You will be able to manage the data of the athletes, of the coaches, you will be able to compose the teams and organize them as you wish.
You will be able to monitor your society on any device: PC, Tablet or phone.

Teams

Compose and manage your teams.

Coaches

Register your coaches and assign them to the team of your choice.

Athletes

Manage every single athlete by registering personal data, payments and medical Certificates.

What to do to get started?

You will need to send us an email containing the username you want to use and the name of your Society. You will be given a password (which you can later modify) and at this point you can start working. In addition to you, you can add other collaborators, that will help you in the management.
